====== CollisionLayers ======
Created Sunday 17 September 2023

In Godot, the Layer is the collision layer the Node exists on, and the Mask is the collision layer the Node collides with

Layers:
1. The Player
2. Enemies
3. Spells

Interactions:
Players mask Enemies
Enemies mask Players and Enemies
Spells mask Enemies
