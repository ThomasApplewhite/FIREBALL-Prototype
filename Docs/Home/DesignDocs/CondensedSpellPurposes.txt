Zap: Quick cooldown generation for minimal damage
Magic Top: High-damage CD consumer to deal with nearby crowds
Ice Spike: Medium-damage CD creator. A better Zap, but consumes health and cools down slower
Twist of Fate: Low-damage (in that it is single target) CD consumer. Heals a lot!
